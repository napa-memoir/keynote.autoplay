THE YEAR A PROMISE WAS MADE

When the giants walked away,
we stepped in.

In the late 1990s, Texas Instruments® and Siemens® discontinued their legendary TI505® and SIMATIC® 505 PLC lines. Thousands of industrial facilities around the world — your facilities — were suddenly left without the support of their original manufacturers.

NAPA International France was founded that year, by former executives from those very same companies. People who knew these systems inside out — and who believed that the factories relying on them deserved a reliable partner, not an exit notice.

Our mission from day one: protect what you built. Keep it running. Keep it modern. Never force you to stop your lines.

We will never abandon the systems that keep your production lines alive — no matter how old, no matter how complex.

The journey

Nearly three decades of milestones.

1998
NAPA International France is founded
Former TI® and Siemens® executives launch NAPA with one mission: be the industrial automation partner that legacy PLC customers never had — expert, loyal, and unconditionally present.
Early 2000s'
Strategic partnerships: CTI & FasTrak
Alliance with Control Technology Inc. and FasTrak SoftWorks brings a full ecosystem of hardware and software solutions — 100% compatible with the TI505® and SIMATIC® 505 installed base.
2000s' – 2010s'
Five continents, one commitment
A solid global distribution network takes shape across the Americas, Europe, Asia, Middle-East, Africa and Oceania — bringing NAPA's expertise to factories wherever they operate.
2010s' – 2020s'
Modernization without disruption
The CTI 2500 Series® platform delivers the future : full compatibility with existing I/O wiring, full compatibility with our newly available CPU line, upgrades in minutes — never hours, never days. Production never stops.
2020s' – Today
The new JANUS IEC 61131-3 platform becomes available.
  • New programming environment supporting all IEC 61131-3 languages (SFC, FBD, LD, ST and IL)
  • Up to 10x execution speed compared to current 2500 Series CPUs
  • Extensive built-in communications capabilities, allowing you to communicate with best-in-class I/O, drives and HMI/SCADA, no matter their manufacturer

Yet, as ever —

  • True to our DNA, YOU decide the pace of YOUR evolution. We offer you the choice and the path, but we will never decide when is the best time for your facility to move from one platform to another.
  • Unlike the rest of the industry, we will not make our legacy platform obsolete to force you to adopt & invest in our latest development every 10 years.
  • This has been our definition of the TOTAL COST OF OWNERSHIP over the past 3 decades and this is what really counts in the end.
